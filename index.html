What is this about?

The potato (Solanum tuberosum) belongs to the solanaceae family of flowering plants. It originated and was first domesticated in the Andesmountains of South America.

The potato is the third most important food crop in the world after rice and wheat in terms of human consumption. More than a billion people worldwide eat potato, and global total crop production exceeds 300 million metric tons.


The World of Potatoes is a metaverse world based on the NFT you own. Each NFT guarantees you land in metaverse which will harvest you a certain amount of tokens every day which will be paid to your set wallet.



The potatoes will be converted to 3D and will act as metaverse bodies. Users will be able to interact with each other, harvest the profit from lands or buy even more of them.
